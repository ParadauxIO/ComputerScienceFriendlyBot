*;randomcolor*

Generates a random hexadecimal RGB color.

;serverinfo

Shows information about this guild.

**Set Color**
;setcolor <HEX>

Sets your role color to any arbitrary hexadecimal RGB color. Formatted as 0x000000 or FFFFFF
