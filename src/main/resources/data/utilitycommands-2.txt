**Strike Command**
;strike <message>

Generate a unicode and markdown representation of your message, with a strike through it.

**Tag Command**
;tag <create/delete/view> <id> [content]>

Manage tags, which are special user-defined commands used by beginning with !, think of them like aliases or macros.

**User Information**
*;userinfo*

Shows information about your user.

**Weather Information**
;weather <Location>

Shows weather information pertaining to the location specified.

**Wolfram Alpha: Search**
*;wolframalpha <query>*

Search Wolfram Alpha from the comfort of discord!
